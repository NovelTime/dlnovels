[LN] Drugstore in Another World: The Slow Life of a Cheat Pharmacist

Description

Corporate slave Reiji went to another world one day, made one rank higher potions, and li~ghtly made a killer profit!

He used that money to open a drug store, and troubled people who heard the rumors came over...

Eyedrops for an elf troubled over his failing bow aim! Cough syrup for the dragon that unintentionally releases his dragon breath!

Reiji makes medicine for troubled people, enjoying his leisurely slow life in a rural city.
